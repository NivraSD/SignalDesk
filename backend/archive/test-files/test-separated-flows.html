Implementation Details
AI Assistant (UnifiedPlatform.js):
  • Detects content requests and auto-opens Content Generator
  • Uses mode: 'content' for conversational responses
  • Only passes ACTUAL generated content (validated by isActualGeneratedContent())
  • Never passes conversational responses as content
Content Generator (ContentGeneratorModule.js):
  • Accepts generated content from AI Assistant
  • Maintains its own buttons for manual generation
  • Shows full interface when no content
  • Shows content-only view when content exists
Content Detection Rules:
  • Must include strong indicators ("FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE", "Q:", etc.)
  • Must NOT include questions or conversational markers
  • Must be >400 chars with >5 lines for weak indicators
  • Rejects anything with "What?", "I can help", "tips", etc.

Benefits of Separation
For Conversational Users:
  • Natural dialogue flow
  • AI guides them through the process
  • No need to understand UI buttons
  • Gets personalized content based on conversation
For Power Users:
  • Direct access to generation
  • No conversation needed
  • Faster for those who know what they want
  • Traditional UI they're familiar with
For the System:
  • Clean separation of concerns
  • No confusing mixed states
  • Each flow works independently
  • Easier to maintain and debug
